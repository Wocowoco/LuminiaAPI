 Acquiring Research Points

Whenever you have something that can yield Research Points (given by the DM), you will need to invest some time and money in to gaining that Research Point. This can be done by spending 8 hours on research and spending some money on buying supplies to work with.

The DC is an Alchemist Supplies (Int) skill check. The DC is lowered by the amount of money spent on supplies for the research.

Cost {{roll.price}} DC {{roll.dc}}

If you failed the check, you can retry by putting in the hours and money again until you succeed. Once you do succeed, you can no longer gain Research Points from that specific source.

You can spend a week (7 days) researching without having a specific source to get Research Points from. The costs for materials is multiplied by 7 (materials for each day). On a succesful check at the end of the week, you will gain one Research Point.
